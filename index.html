1. Introduction

MIB2 Controller is a specialized Android application designed for vehicle owners to diagnose, configure, and maintain their Volkswagen Group MIB2 STD2 Technisat/Preh infotainment units. This privacy policy explains our data collection, use, and protection practices.

Key Point: MIB2 Controller does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All diagnostic information remains on your device under your control.

2. Data Collection

2.1 What We DON'T Collect

MIB2 Controller does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts, photos, or media files
  • Payment information or financial data
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Analytics or advertising data

2.2 Local Diagnostic Data

The app stores the following diagnostic information locally on your device only:

  • Device model and Android version: For compatibility diagnostics
  • Connection timestamps: When you connect to your MIB2 unit
  • MIB2 unit identifiers (VIN, VCRN): Only if you manually enter them for FEC code generation
  • USB adapter information: VID/PID values of connected adapters
  • Network diagnostic output: Ping times, IP addresses on the local USB-Ethernet link
  • Telnet session logs: Commands you execute and their responses

Important: All of this data is stored in your device's app-private storage (Android sandbox) and is never transmitted to external servers, third parties, or the app developer.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only

All diagnostic logs and configuration data are stored locally on your Android device in the app's private storage directory. This data is protected by Android's application sandbox security model and is not accessible to other apps or external parties.

3.2 User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export logs: You can manually export diagnostic logs using the "Share" button, which uses Android's standard share sheet. You choose where to send the logs (email, cloud storage, etc.).
  • Clear data: You can delete all stored logs and configuration data at any time using the in-app "Clear Data" button in Settings.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

3.3 No Cloud Sync

MIB2 Controller does not include cloud synchronization, backup services, or remote data storage. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

4.1 USB Permission (Required)

Purpose: To detect and communicate with USB-Ethernet adapters connected via OTG cable.
Disclosure: Explained in the in-app FAQ and store listing.
Sensitive: No

4.2 USB Host Feature (Optional)

Purpose: Declares USB Host capability. Marked as android:required="false" to allow installation on devices without USB Host support.
Disclosure: Declared in AndroidManifest.xml.
Sensitive: No

4.3 POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Required on Android 13+)

Purpose: To display connection status notifications and diagnostic alerts.
Disclosure: Prompted by Android system when first needed.
Sensitive: No

4.4 INTERNET (Implicit)

Purpose: Required for local TCP/IP communication with the MIB2 unit over the USB-Ethernet adapter. The app does NOT access external internet resources.
Disclosure: Explained in this privacy policy.
Sensitive: No

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 No Analytics or Tracking

MIB2 Controller does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking services. There are no embedded trackers, cookies, or fingerprinting mechanisms.

5.2 External Links

The app includes a link to an external FEC code generator (vwcoding.ru). When you click this link, you leave the app and are subject to that website's privacy policy. We do not control or take responsibility for external websites.

6. Children's Privacy

MIB2 Controller is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us (though note that we do not collect personal information from any users).

7. Data Retention

Diagnostic logs and configuration data are retained locally on your device until you:

  • Manually delete them using the in-app "Clear Data" button
  • Clear the app's data through Android Settings → Apps → MIB2 Controller → Storage → Clear Data
  • Uninstall the app

We do not have access to your data, so we cannot delete it remotely. You have complete control.

8. International Users

MIB2 Controller operates entirely on your local device. No data is transmitted internationally or stored on servers in any jurisdiction. All data processing occurs locally on your Android device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying an in-app notification when you next open the app

Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as:

  • Right to access: You can access all your data directly within the app (Settings → View Logs)
  • Right to deletion: You can delete all your data at any time (Settings → Clear Data)
  • Right to portability: You can export your data using the "Share" button
  • Right to object: You can stop using the app at any time and uninstall it

Since we do not collect or store personal data on external servers, these rights are automatically fulfilled through the app's local data management features.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing any data is:

  • Legitimate interests: To provide diagnostic and configuration functionality for your vehicle's infotainment system
  • Consent: By using the app, you consent to the local storage of diagnostic logs on your device

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect. As stated above, we do not collect personal information.
  • Right to delete: You have the right to request deletion of personal information. Since data is stored locally, you can delete it directly through the app.
  • Right to opt-out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
